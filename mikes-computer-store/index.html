why buy from Mike's Computer?

If you're searching for the cheapest Gaming PC made with inferior products and questionable quality, you might as well leave now. Mike's Computer only uses the highest quality performance Gaming PC components available. Every single customized Gaming PC is assembled right here in the United States by highly trained and incredibly skilled technicians and assembly engineers. Sure, we can assemble our award-winning Gaming PCs overseas for less. In fact, that's what most of our competitors do. However, we choose to assemble all our Gaming PCs right here in the United States because it gives us the best control over manufacturing, performance, and overall quality.

You see, at Mike's Computer, our customers' long-term satisfaction is the most important thing for us. We want our customers to be fully satisfied with their ownership experience from beginning to end. And not just satisfied within the first 90 days when everything is shiny and new, but very satisfied years later, even as new and more advanced gaming software is introduced. And if something happens to your PC years later, we don't abandon you. We have a highly trained support team always available to help out our wonderful family of Mike's Computer owners. Again. We're committed to your long-term ownership experience.

To answer the original question, why should you buy a new Gaming Rig from Mike's Computer? It's the commitment to quality: That includes the best components, best technicians, best quality control, best support, and ultimately best performance that keeps our customers coming back to purchase again and again.

You can always pay less for an inferior Computer Tower that was assembled in massive quantities by tired factory workers in a faraway country. And when it eventually breaks, you find out that your support has expired and the company will no longer stand behind the Gaming System you paid for. Ouch!

Of course, everyone prefers getting the best of the best. But we all know, quality, performance, and support costs more. However, that's not entirely true. See, when you build the highest quality PCs with the highest quality components, a funny thing happens. We get less returns. We get less warranty claims. We get more repeat customers. That means, our prices are astonishingly very competitive for both our pre-built Gaming Computers and our Custom Built Gaming PCs.
